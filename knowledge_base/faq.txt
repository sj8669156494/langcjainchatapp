Q: How do I set up HomeBuddy?
A: To set up HomeBuddy, download the app from your device's app store, create an account, and follow the in-app instructions to connect your smart devices.

Q: Is HomeBuddy compatible with my existing smart devices?
A: HomeBuddy is compatible with most major smart home brands. Check our website for a full list of supported devices.

Q: How much does HomeBuddy cost?
A: HomeBuddy has a one-time cost of $99 for the hardware, with no additional subscription fees.
